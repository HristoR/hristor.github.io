Project Overview

The Challenge

Leading a 10-person multidisciplinary team developing on a custom engine without dedicated artists. Required establishing robust production pipelines, coordinating external art resources, and maintaining team velocity while managing technical constraints inherent to custom engine development.

My Role as Lead Producer

  • Vision holder and production leadership
  • Established dual-tool AGILE workflow
  • Coordinated cross-functional teams (Design, Programming)
  • Managed external artist relationships
  • Facilitated daily standups and sprint planning

Key Production Achievements

Workflow Innovation

  • Implemented dual-tool system combining GitHub Projects for technical tracking and Codecks for design workflow
  • Created custom templates for sprint planning adapted to custom engine constraints
  • Established clear communication protocols between programmers and external artists

Team Coordination

  • Led daily 15-minute standups maintaining team alignment across 10 members
  • Facilitated weekly sprint reviews and retrospectives
  • Coordinated external artist integration without disrupting internal development flow

Production Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Managing Without Dedicated Artists

The team lacked internal art resources, creating potential bottlenecks for visual asset delivery and integration.

Solution

Established relationships with external artists and created detailed asset specifications with technical requirements. Implemented a review pipeline that allowed quick iteration without disrupting development sprints.

Challenge: Custom Engine Technical Constraints

Working with a custom engine meant dealing with unexpected technical limitations and lack of standard documentation.

Solution

Created comprehensive internal documentation for engine workflows. Implemented tech debt tracking within sprint planning that balanced new features with engine stability improvements.
